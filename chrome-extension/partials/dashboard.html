How to install?

  • First, set your profile public. The extension cannot access your information if your profile is private.
  • Then, acces your profile page in the browser while logged in. The extension will detect your profile and then will fetch your game list automatically.
  • Worried about your account security? The extension source code is available to everyone on Github.
  • Your backlog is not shared between browsers. So you can only browse your backlog in this computer.
Note: This extension uses the Steam API to get your games and its related information. If you reach the limit of usage you may need to wait a little before continue using the extension.
